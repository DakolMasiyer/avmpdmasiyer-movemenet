About the Candidate

A Soldier Returning to Serve

Air Vice Marshal Paul D. Masiyer (Rtd.) is a distinguished son of Mangu/Bokkos Federal Constituency, Plateau State. Over a career spanning more than three decades in the Nigerian Air Force, he rose to the rank of Air Vice Marshal — the peak of commissioned service.

His most senior appointments — Chief of Logistics at HQ NAF and Commandant of the Military Training Centre — required the kind of precision, discipline, and integrity that he now brings to this campaign.

Retirement from the military did not mean retirement from service. It meant finding a new way to serve — starting with the people of Mangu/Bokkos.

Campaign Platform

Our 8-Pillar Agenda

Eight commitments — each grounded in the real needs of Mangu/Bokkos communities. Not vague aspirations. Specific, accountable plans.

🛡️

Security & Lasting Peace

Addressing farmer-herder conflict and building lasting community safety as the foundation for all other progress.

🌾

Farming & Local Business

Federal support for smallholder farmers, market access roads, and agricultural livelihoods across both LGAs.

🎓

Education & Healthcare

Quality schools and functional health facilities in every ward in the constituency — no exceptions.

💼

Youth Empowerment

Skills, employment, and entrepreneurship opportunities so young people build Mangu/Bokkos — not leave it.

🛣️

Economic & Physical Development

Roads, utilities, and federal infrastructure investment directed to the constituency with accountability.

👩‍👧

Women, Elderly & Vulnerable

Targeted empowerment and federal programme access for those most often overlooked in national policy.

🥁

Cultural Heritage

Fostering and exporting the rich cultural identity of Mangu/Bokkos — Mwaghavul, Challa, and all our peoples.

📢

A Voice at the National Stage

Ensuring Mangu/Bokkos is heard — and heard clearly — when federal decisions are made in Abuja.
